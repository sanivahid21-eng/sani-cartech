Things worth asking

What does a car electrical diagnostic actually involve?
It involves connecting a diagnostic scan tool, reading fault codes from all modules, capturing live sensor data, and where needed using an oscilloscope to verify signal integrity. The aim is identifying the actual cause of the symptom — which is often different from what a single fault code suggests.
How much does a car electrical diagnostic cost in Ocean Grove?
Standard car electrical diagnostic cost ranges from $160–$280 depending on vehicle complexity. Mobile call-outs to Ocean Grove are $220–$380. Once the fault is identified, the repair quote is separate and based on parts and labour required.

Why does my check engine light keep coming back?
Recurring check engine lights usually mean the underlying fault wasn’t fully resolved. Either the wrong part was replaced, or there’s an intermittent issue that surface-level scanning missed. Proper diagnostics with live data capture is the way through.
Can you find the cause of an intermittent electrical fault?
Yes — but it sometimes takes more than one visit. Intermittent faults need logged scanning, drive-test data capture, and occasionally an oscilloscope left connected to monitor a specific circuit until the fault triggers again.
